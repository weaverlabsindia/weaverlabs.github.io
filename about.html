About the Consultancy

Founded by a researcher with deep expertise in Supercapacitor and battery cell fabrication and characterization, extensive knowledge of underlying electrochemistry and numerical calculations using finite element method (FEM), we strive to advance electrochemical energy-storage technology for Indian and global industry and research through:

  • Consultancy on new product development
  • Computational calculations to reduce time needed for device development and deployment, and to find potential flaws
  • Starter/refresher workshops and courses in supercapacitor/battery fabrication and testing

We bring to you:

  • Over 10 Years of experience in supercapacitor and battery research and development
  • Expertise in electrode synthesis and testing, cell fabrication and electrochemical characterization
  • Expertise in Python, Finite Element Method
